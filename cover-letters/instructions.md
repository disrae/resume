# Cover Letter Generation Instructions

## Tone & Voice

First-person, authentic, passionate about technology and problem-solving. Be
extremely brief and make it sounds honest, human, natural.

## The Recipe (Cover Letter Ingredients Formula)

Pull specific examples and achievements from experience-tracker.md. Focus on:

- Quantifiable results and impact
- Technical skills relevant to the role
- Problem-solving and innovation

## Output Requirements

Clean text file (.txt) with professional formatting. File naming:
`Daniel Israel - Company Name Cover Letter.txt`
